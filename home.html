This is pool50! Although we do not currently have a fully functioning multiplayer pool game, we have smaller, but still engaging, tasks for you. Feel free to browse through the different tabs we have available to practice your pool skills. Right now, we have the following tasks: get a cue ball into a pocket, get a 8 ball into a pocket, use the cue ball to get another ball into a pocket, and explore how hitting the cue ball breaks up a ball setup. We hope you have a fun time as you browse through our pool game! If you need any reminders about how pool works or how our implementation works, feel free to look at the isntructions tab.
